# Juniper Loop: Lightweight Brand Kit

## Voice

Juniper Loop sounds calm, encouraging, useful, and grounded. The brand should feel like a steady companion, not a coach with a whistle or a medical expert.

## Copy Principles

- Lead with usefulness, not pressure.
- Make next steps feel small and doable.
- Keep messages direct, human, and low-pressure.
- Avoid perfection language, guilt, fear, and body-performance framing.
- Prefer everyday routines over transformation promises.
- Keep personalization respectful and explainable.

## Do

- "A small reset for the week ahead."
- "Pick one routine to make tomorrow easier."
- "Three minutes, one useful check-in."
- "Your evening wind-down, simplified."
- "A gentle way back into your routine."

## Do Not

- "Fix your body in 7 days."
- "We know why you are stressed."
- "You failed your routine again."
- "This will treat your symptoms."
- "Based on your medical profile..."

## Experience Ingredients

- Starter routines.
- Short articles, audio, and video.
- Community prompts and challenges.
- Product bundles that support everyday routines.
- Preference check-ins.
- Progress moments and reflections.

## Creative Guardrails

- Do not imply diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.
- Do not invent sensitive health attributes.
- Do not use shame, fear, or perfection as motivation.
- Do not make guaranteed outcome claims.
- Do not send messages through channels where consent is missing.

## Optional Visual Assets

Teams may use the lightweight Juniper Loop asset kit in `../brand_assets/`. It includes editable logos, CSS tokens, sample HTML templates, and local lifestyle/product images. These assets are a starting point, not a required design system.

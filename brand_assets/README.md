# Juniper Loop Brand Assets

This lightweight kit gives teams a shared starting point for Juniper Loop demos. It is optional: teams may extend or reinterpret the assets as long as they stay aligned with the Juniper Loop brand voice and guardrails.

## Brand Idea

Juniper Loop is an everyday-habits membership app and commerce brand. The brand should feel calm, useful, grounded, and human without becoming clinical, preachy, or overly optimized.

## Files

- `logo/juniper-loop-lockup.svg`: editable logo lockup for headers, slides, and demos.
- `logo/juniper-loop-mark.svg`: standalone mark for app icons, badges, and small UI surfaces.
- `css/juniper-loop-tokens.css`: reusable CSS variables and base utility styles.
- `templates/lifecycle-email.html`: sample lifecycle email starter.
- `templates/app-moment-card.html`: sample app moment card for routine or content nudges.
- `templates/web-experience.html`: sample web/app hero for prototype demos.
- `images/*.png`: editorial product moment images for mockups and presentation use.

## Typography

Use a humanist sans-serif for UI and body copy, with a warm serif only for occasional display moments. Suggested stacks are defined in `css/juniper-loop-tokens.css` and require no external font files.

## Usage Guidance

- Keep customer-facing copy editable outside images.
- Use the images as atmosphere, routine context, or product inspiration, not as proof of real merchandise.
- Avoid medical claims, sensitive-health implications, and perfection language.
- Prefer gentle, useful next steps over pressure or transformation promises.
